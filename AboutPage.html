About Rabbit House

Rabbit House is a cafe that serves homemade drinks like coffee, tea and shakes. Each drink is made using freshly picked ingredients to bring the best tasting drinks for the customers. The cafe also offers a chill and relaxed atmosphere for the customers to appreciate as they stay.

About the Drinks

The drinks that are available are coffee, tea and shake. These are also homemade drinks that is made from the best quality coffee beans, tea leaves and fruits.
