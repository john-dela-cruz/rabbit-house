Tea

Tea is a drink that is very aromatic and it can be sour and sweet to spicy and earthy.

  • Jasmine Tea
  • Green Tea
  • Chamomile Tea

Jasmine Tea

This tea is a drink that is scented with the aroma of the jasmine flower and it typically has the base of a green tea but sometimes it uses the black or white tea instead. This drink has a sweet and floral aroma that then complements the bitterness of the base tea.

Green Tea

This tea is made from the leaves of the camellia sinensis plant. The process in making this tea is that the leaves of the plant are plucked, slighly withered and then it would be cooked to preserve the green quality and prevent oxidization.

Chamomile Tea

This tea is made from the chamomile flower and this drink has a wide range of health benefits. This drink has a honey-like sweetness taste. This drink offers benefits like lowering blood sugar, treating cold symptoms, helping with sleep and etc.
