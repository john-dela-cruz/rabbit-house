Shake

Shake is a drink that is consumed cold and it typically has a sweet flavor.

  • Strawberry Banana
  • Raspberry Almond
  • Chocolate Peanut Butter

Strawberry Banana

This shake can be made using almond milk, banana, coconut oil and strawberry.

Raspberry Almond

This shake can be made using raspberry, almond and honey.

Chocolate Peanut Butter

This shake can be made using vanilla ice cream, milk, chocolate syprup, peanut butter and whipped cream.
