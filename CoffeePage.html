Coffee

Coffee is a drink that is darkly colored, bitter and a drink that has caffeine which can have stimulating effect for the consumers.

  • Americano
  • Espresso
  • Latte

Americano

This coffee is a drink that is prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water. It has a similar strength to brewed coffee but is different from flavor and its strength would vary depending on the number of espresso shots and amount of water that is added to the drink.

Espresso

This coffee is a drink that can be made with a variety of coffee beans and roast degrees. This drink is thicker than coffee brewed in other ways, with having the same viscosity of a warm honey.

Latte

This coffee is a drink is made by combining espresso and milk. The milk that could be used in the mixture besides the regular milk are soy milk or almond milkThis drink is mostly prepared at home for breakfast.
